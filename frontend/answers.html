  1. Ryan: If you have solved Puzzle A, Mary Ann will tell you about a Caesar cipher. The letters are encoded through this type of cipher. Ryan’s prompts will indicate that he is numbering each guest table. Thus, the number of guest tables (31) is the key to unlock the cipher. If you shift each letter 31 positions back up the alphabet, then you will arrive at the letters that spell out the answer, getaway.

  4. Tim: If you have solved Puzzle B, the pictures on the safe buttons will turn into Chinese characters. When pronouncing these Chinese characters, you will find that certain ones sound very close to the characters used in Matt’s name. In Cantonese, only the words for “bok choy”, “cooked”, and “green onion” sound very close to Matt’s name. These three keys correspond to the solution of buttons 3, 4, and 9.
  5. Helena: Each bridal party member holds a piece of the paper grid. When you put these pieces together correctly, you will see the original full grid with some Xs. If you head over to the photobooth, you will see a prop with a Scrabble-like grid. Overlap the grid from the puzzle with the grid from the photobooth prop and you will find letters marked by the spaces with an X. These letters will unscramble to spell out the answer, devotion.
